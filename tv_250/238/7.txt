Excellent portrayal of a forgotten subculture. When I first saw the film 'Paris is Burning' in 1990, I never would have imagined a dramatization of that world making it one day to series television. The dialogue is a little melodramatic and the story somewhat contrived at times, but not so much that it overshadowed the wonderful characters and exciting ball competition sequences.
